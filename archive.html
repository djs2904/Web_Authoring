Week 2
A Week In My Life

Preface

This week, I decided to document the trash I accumulated throughout the week. I was surprised by the amount of trash I produced and the types of items I threw away. I hope this exercise will help me become more conscious of my waste and encourage me to reduce, reuse, and recycle.

Overview

I'm hungry

  • I relied heavily on coffee pods for my daily caffeine fix, resulting in a significant amount ofwaste.

  • Do you know what goes well with coffee? Cookies. Specifically, Oreo cookies. I went through an entire packet of Oreos in one week.

  • I enjoyed a late night ice cream in a disposable glass.

A lot of things broke last night

  • I accidentally broke a clothes hanger while trying to hang up my laundry.

  • I accidentally snapped my debit card while making a purchase.

  • My phone charger cable stopped working, and I had to throw it away.

Staying healthy and groomed

  • I used up an entire bottle of sunscreen while spending time outdoors.

  • I finished a packet of vitamin pills this week.

  • I went through several paper towels while cleaning up spills and messes.
